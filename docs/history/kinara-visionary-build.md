Display this as a beautiful scrollable panel that opens when you tap on a connection line in the comparison wheel. Each interpretation should be 3-5 sentences — meaningful, not generic.

- For each day: the tone's action/power/essence keywords AND a short practitioner note — "Today is about [tone keyword]. In your sound healing sessions, focus on [specific guidance mapped to the tone's musical interval from Book 2]."
- A mini calendar showing all 13 days of the current Wavespell with their Kin, seal icon, and tone

This is the **daily engagement hook** — practitioners check this every morning to align their sessions with the galactic timing.
